FAQ

Everything people ask
before they switch.

Will my site go down during migration?+

No. We build a full copy of your site on our platform while your current host keeps serving visitors. You test the copy, and only when you're satisfied do you point DNS at us. There is no moment where your site is offline.

Is the migration really free? What's the catch?+

Really free, for any number of sites, on any plan. The "catch" is our business model: once you experience the speed difference, you stay. The 30-day guarantee means the risk of finding out is entirely ours.

What happens when my traffic spikes?+

Your site stays up. Shared plans include burst capacity, and VPS resources resize live without a reboot. We notify you about sustained growth and recommend a plan — we never throttle you into a sales call.

Do you lock me in with contracts or proprietary tech?+

No. Standard stack (Linux, LiteSpeed/NGINX, MySQL/MariaDB), full backups you can download anytime, and no exit fees. We'd rather earn your renewal than trap it — that's also why we help you move back if you leave within the guarantee window.

What does the 99.99% uptime SLA actually mean?+

It's contractual, not decorative: if measured monthly uptime falls below 99.99%, you're automatically credited per our SLA — you don't have to notice, file, or fight for it.

How fast is support, honestly?+

Live chat median first response is under 2 minutes, around the clock. Every agent is a systems engineer with server access — the person who answers is the person who fixes.
