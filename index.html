06 — FAQ

Questions we get every time.

How much does an AI product cost to build?+

A one-week feasibility audit is a fixed, small engagement. An MVP sprint is typically a 4–8 week fixed-scope project. Full platforms like Morpheus OS are phased. You always know the number before we start — no hourly meters running in the dark.

We're not sure AI is even the right solution. Can you tell us?+

Yes — and we will, honestly. The feasibility audit exists precisely for this. Roughly a third of ideas we audit don't need AI, and we say so. The ones that do, get a working prototype as proof.

Which AI models and stack do you use?+

Whatever serves the product: frontier APIs (Claude, GPT), open-weight models when privacy or cost demands it, classic ML when LLMs are overkill. The stack is modern and boring on purpose — your product shouldn't be a science experiment.

Who owns the code and the data?+

You do. Full repository handover, infrastructure in your accounts, documentation included. We design for your independence, then earn the right to keep working with you.

Do you work with existing teams or only greenfield?+

Both. We've embedded with in-house teams to add AI capability, and we've built products solo from zero. We adapt to your process — weekly demos stay non-negotiable either way.
